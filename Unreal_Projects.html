Areiazo

VR Puzzle Game (Spring 2020)

Areiazo is a class project made using Unreal and the Advanced VR Framework.

Description

You are a traveler passing through, until you come across a deserted kingdom. Here you find a cute little ghost, stuck atop a massive head statue. Solve puzzles and travel with this cute ghost, as you uncover the long-forgotten secrets of Areiazo.
