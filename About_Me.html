About Raphy

Aspiring for life goals.

I am currently a student attending California. I founded Team SHMUP and am currently working on our game RoVo, as well as our next game, Rage Room VR.

Why Game Development and Programming?

Much of my inspirations for Game Development started very young. My earliest memories involve watching my brother play fantastical real-time-strategy games like Homeworld and Dawn of War. I later went on my own path towards Games, enjoying many console titles such as the Assassin's Creed and Halo franchises.

When I entered college and learned how to program, I was naive about how programming fit into my goals to create games. As time went on, however, my attitude changed and my passion grew. I discovered how programming itself was an art, that took discipline and creativity. This helped bolster my skills as I progressed my work into Unity, and later Unreal.

Now, I enjoy the thrill of problem-solving involved with Programming, and how I can apply it to Game Development. This is now my art, my craft, my present and future.
